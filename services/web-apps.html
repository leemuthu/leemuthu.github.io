A web app is different from a website: it's an interactive tool, not just a page people read. Client portals, internal dashboards, booking systems, inventory trackers. If your business needs a tool built around a specific workflow instead of a static page, that's a web application.

We build custom web apps for businesses and startups who need something a template can't provide. Every app is built around your actual process: how your team or your customers will use it day to day, not a generic feature list. You get a clean, functional tool and the source code to go with it.

What counts as a web app instead of a website?

A website mostly displays information. A web app lets people do something: log in, submit data, view a dashboard, manage bookings. If your project involves user accounts, data that changes based on who's using it, or an interactive tool your team relies on, you're looking at a web app.

What kind of web apps do you build?

Internal dashboards, client portals, booking and scheduling tools, inventory and project trackers, and early stage product prototypes for startups. If you have a specific workflow that needs a proper interface, tell us what it is and we'll tell you what's possible.

How long does a web app take to build?

Timelines depend heavily on scope. A simple single page tool might take about a week. A full dashboard with multiple views and integrations takes longer. We'll give you a realistic timeline once we understand exactly what you need the app to do.

Frequently asked questions

What technologies do you build with?

We build with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React depending on the complexity of the project. We use the right tool for what you actually need, not the most complicated option available.

Can you integrate with tools I already use?

Yes. We can connect your app to third party tools and APIs like Airtable, Google Sheets, Stripe, and others depending on your project.

Do you build the backend too?

We can, depending on scope. Tell us what your app needs to do and we'll tell you exactly what's required to make it work.

Can this scale as my business grows?

We build with your current needs in mind but structure the code so it isn't a dead end if you need to expand later.

Do you offer support after launch?

Yes. We offer ongoing support packages for clients who want updates, fixes, or new features after the initial build.
